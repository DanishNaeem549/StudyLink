✦ FAQ

Questions, answered

Our AI looks at your enrolled courses, past exam scores, learning style preferences, and availability — then ranks tutors by a match score that factors in their subject expertise, GPA, teaching reviews, and schedule overlap. You always see why a tutor was recommended.
Every tutor is verified through their university email and academic transcript. We confirm their GPA (minimum 3.5), and they must complete a short onboarding quiz for each subject they teach. After each session, students rate tutors — low-rated tutors are automatically paused.
Yes — you can cancel or reschedule any session up to 2 hours before it starts with no penalty. Late cancellations may use one of your monthly session slots. We always send you a reminder 24 hours and 1 hour before each session.
Tutors earn StudyLink Points for every completed session, which can be redeemed for cash, university bookstore credits, or platform credits. Top-rated tutors unlock bonus multipliers. Premium tutors (PKR 999/mo) also get priority student requests and higher payout rates.
We launched in Pakistan and currently serve 40+ universities across Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad. Global expansion is underway — if your university isn't listed, you can join the waitlist and we'll notify you when we go live in your city.
